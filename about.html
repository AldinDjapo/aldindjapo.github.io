Djapo is a musician, guitarist, producer and a DJ, born in Germany in 1997. Raised in Bosnia and Herzegovina, he graduated from International University of Sarajevo, department Software Engineering. Alongside his studies, he was a coach in the most successful swimming club in Bosnian history. Sadly, that bond was broken the day he got accepted for the masters degree at KunstUni in Linz, department "Postdigital Lutherie". He graduated from Primary Musical School Ilidza in Bosnia where he played piano.


Not so long after that he gained interest in guitar, specifically electric guitar mostly because of his favourite band "Metallica". Soon after he joins the band "Radar" where he is currently playing and writing their first album. He also gained interest in music production, specifically techno genre. Until this day he had several releases for "Minimalism Records", "Prospect Records" and "Ambi Records". Currently he is mostly focused on his Software Engineering career and developing his programming skills in the pursuit of a better future.
